Future Plans

Expanding the number of subjects

Our website is currently focused on providing resources for a select number of subjects, but we plan to expand the number of subjects we cover in the future. This will allow us to better serve the needs of a wider range of students and provide more comprehensive study materials

Introducing Online Tests

We plan to introduce online tests in the future, which will allow students to test their knowledge and assess their performance in a particular subject. These tests will be timed, and the results will be presented to the students immediately after completing the test.

Personalized Time Table Creator

We plan to develop a personalized time table creator for students, which will allow them to create a customized study schedule that fits their individual needs and preferences. This will help them to stay organized and stay on track with their studies.

Collaborative Study Platform

We plan to develop a collaborative study platform, where students can interact and work together on assignments, projects, and group studies. This will encourage students to share their knowledge and learning resources with each other, and will help to build a community of learners.

About Us

Ajmer Ka Student is a website that was created with a goal of providing comprehensive study materials for students in schools and colleges in Ajmer. The creator of this website, a student of BSc 3rd year, understands the importance of having access to quality study materials and the struggles of exam preparation. That's why the website was created, to make it easier for students to find the resources they need to excel in their exams
